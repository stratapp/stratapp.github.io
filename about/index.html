we are changing how leaders think about, share and collaborate on executing strategy and day-to-day work

bringing all levels, roles and generations on the same journey

we are experienced SaaS founders, with a 10 year vision backed by investors who are market leaders

For 30 years strategy formation, documentation, communication and execution has been flawed. The age old paradigms of static documents and top down control are holding us back. It is a global problem, affecting most organizations regardless of size, sector, culture or country.

It is time we fixed this.

We embraced the progressive thinking in social and work collaboration tech of the last 11 years, to create a seamless experience for executing both strategy and day-to-day work, in one place.

We did not invent new management buzz words, theory or methods. We carefully designed #stratapp around what you already know.

Therefore, your time-to-value on #stratapp is days - not months, quarters or years.

we are rethinking SaaS playbooks on time-to-value

B2B SaaS companies typically lock their clients into a prepaid contract, then leave the clients to figure out implementation by themselves; often putting forward a list of ‘preferred partners’ offering different advice, methodologies, resources and pricing.

We are rethinking the SaaS playbook. We designed the methodology for our partners AND we prescribed reasonable cost budgets for each and every step.

Why? We genuinely care about our clients’ success, and that includes removing the stress, hassle and unforseen costs of implementation.

Your time-to-value on #stratapp is days - not months, quarters or years.

three big changes
we believe 3 big changes are coming in the next 5 years
  • 1
    a new B2B software category is coming
    Nearly every organization globally with 75+ employees will adopt SaaS to collaborate and execute on strategy AND day-to-day work - in one place, in one seamless user experience.
    We don’t go to work to do CRM, HCM, finance and email - these are important, but secondary.
    We go to work to collaborate and execute on our organization’s strategy and plans - that is what matters.
    #stratapp is the world’s first SaaS to rise to this new B2B software category.
  • 2
    companies will rationalize the proliferation of single-use apps
    Consumers are organizing their smartphones around the apps they really need - less is more.
    CEOs and business owners don’t want to send employees to 10+ different apps in order to do their jobs.
    #stratapp is 10-apps-in-1.
  • 3
    the brilliant and viral evolution of work collaboration tech (e.g. basecamp, trello, jira and asana) are only known to about 10-15% of employees - generally folks working in digital, product or technology teams
    Why? For 11 years, the design of social and work collaboration tech has focused on small teams up to 30 people.
    #stratapp is designed for all levels, roles and generations - scalable org-wide from 5 to 500,000 employees.
    #stratapp delivers genuine org-wide collaboration - reducing the noise, by framing collaboration within a relevant context of strategy and plans.
    #stratapp is a game changer.
